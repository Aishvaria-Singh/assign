Bhupen Hazarika Setu-Dhola-Sadiya Bridge

The Dhola-Sadiya also refered to as the Bhupen Hazarika Setu, a beam bridge in India, connecting the northeast states of Assam and Arunachal Pradesh. The bridge spans the Lohit River, a majority tributary of the mighty Brahmaputra River, from the village od Dhola (Tinsukia District) in the South to Sadiya to the north.
