Padmini resort

Hushed amidst greens and nature away from the city, this hotel serves guests with a rejuvenating environment. It is an ideal city escape resort to spend a balmy summer weekend with friends and family. Padmini resort is accessible from Dibrugarh Airport (45km), New Tinsukia railway station (9km).

Padmini resort offers well-appointed rooms intact with all the amenities and an outdoor swimming pool simply adds a bonus.
